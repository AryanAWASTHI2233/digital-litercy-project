Task 1: Infographic (Report)

For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. I selected a vertical infographic layout because it is easy to read and visually organized. In my design, I included important topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and basic email etiquette. I tried to keep the content simple and clear so that anyone can quickly understand the message.

While working on this, I explored different templates and customized them according to my needs by adjusting colors, fonts, and icons. One thing I found interesting was how visual elements like icons and layout can make information more engaging compared to plain text. However, I also faced some difficulty in balancing the design, especially while choosing the right color combination and maintaining proper spacing.

Overall, this task helped me improve my creativity and design skills. It also gave me a better understanding of how to present important information in a visually effective way.
