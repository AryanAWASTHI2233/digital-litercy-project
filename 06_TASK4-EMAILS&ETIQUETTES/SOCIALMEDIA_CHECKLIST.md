{{{Responsible Social Media Use - Checklist
for College Students}}}

 #Do’s:

Think before you post - ask yourself if it reflects 
your personality in a positive way.

Keep your accounts secure by using strong passwords
and privacy settings.

Share useful, respectful, and meaningful content that 
adds value.

Be mindful of your digital footprint - what you post 
today can affect future opportunities.

Use social media for learning, networking, and staying 
updated with academic or career-related content.

#Don’ts:

Don’t share personal or sensitive information (like address,
phone number, passwords).

Don’t engage in online arguments, hate speech, or offensive comments.

Don’t spread fake news or forward information without verifying it.

Don’t waste excessive time scrolling - it can affect 
your productivity and studies.

Don’t post anything that could harm your reputation or
someone else’s feelings.
